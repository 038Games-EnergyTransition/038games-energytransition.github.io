Detailed Description

The station gate. Manages the opening and closing of the station gate. When the player/NPC enters the area, the gate opens in opposite direction and holds it open in that direction until the player exits the area. When the player/NPC enters the area from the other side, the gate opens in the opposite direction and holds it open in that direction until the player exits the area.
